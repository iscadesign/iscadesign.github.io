Community Fitness Snevern

Branding · 2024 · Art Direction, Identity Design, Illustration

The goal was to create a new vibrant look that represents a fusion of the type of training "Crosstraining", the strong connection with the community and a local identity to embody the essence of the brand. One standout feature was the mascot, a regional beloved symbol "The Heidschnucke" that captures the commitment to a healthy lifestyle, performance improvement and fun vibe of the brands character.
